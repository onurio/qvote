Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2025

Hi there! QV is a personal project created to make quadratic voting more accessible to everyone. This privacy policy is simple and straightforward because I believe in transparency.

I'm building this tool because I'm passionate about better decision-making processes, not to collect or use your data.

My Approach to Your Data

QV is designed to be minimally invasive. I only store what's absolutely necessary for the app to function:

The Technical Stuff

When you add QV to Slack, the app needs:

  • Your Slack workspace ID and name (so votes show up in the right place)
  • Access tokens (so QV can post messages and create votes)
  • User IDs (to know who's voting on what)

Vote Information

The app also stores:

  • The votes you create (questions, options, etc.)
  • How people vote (their credit allocations)
  • Basic usage data to help me fix bugs

How I Use This Information

Your data is only used to:

  • Make the voting app work properly in your Slack
  • Let you and your team see voting results
  • Fix problems if something breaks
  • Occasionally improve the app based on how people use it

That's it! I don't analyze your voting patterns, sell information to third parties, or use your data for anything beyond making QV work well.

Data Retention

QV retains data for the following periods:

  • Workspace information (ID, name, access tokens): For as long as you have QV installed in your workspace
  • Voting data (questions, options): For 180 days after the vote is completed
  • Individual votes: These are always anonymized and cannot be traced back to specific users
  • Usage logs: For 30 days to help identify and fix technical issues (contain no personal information)

When you uninstall QV from your Slack workspace, I will delete all associated workspace information within 30 days. Any aggregated voting statistics are fully anonymized and may be retained for improving the service.

Sharing Your Information

I don't share or sell your information to anyone. Period. The only exceptions would be:

  • If I need to use a service provider to help run the technical parts of QV
  • If I'm legally required to share information

Security

I do my best to keep your information secure with reasonable precautions and security features. I'm always working to improve security as the project develops.

Your Data Rights

QV does not store personal data beyond basic workspace information and anonymized voting data. However, you still have the right to request deletion of any data associated with your workspace. If you wish to make such a request, please email omrinuri@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request" and include:

  • Your Slack workspace name
  • Workspace ID (if you know it)

I will respond to your request within 30 days and completely remove all data associated with your workspace from my systems.

Changes to This Policy

If I make any significant changes to this policy, I'll update the date at the top and do my best to let you know.

Questions?

If you have any questions or just want to chat about quadratic voting, feel free to email me at omrinuri@gmail.com. I'd love to hear from you!
